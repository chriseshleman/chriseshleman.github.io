I can also plot the same locations scaled by the number of spots ("seats"). 

```r
plot_scaled = ggplot() +  
  geom_polygon(data=tracts, aes(x=long, y=lat, group=group), fill="grey90", 
               colour="grey90", alpha=1) + labs(x="", y="", title="") + 
  theme(axis.ticks.y = element_blank(),axis.text.y = element_blank(), 
        axis.ticks.x = element_blank(),axis.text.x = element_blank()) +
  geom_point(aes(x=x, y=y, size=(seats), color=seats), data=upk, alpha=1) + #, color="grey20") +
  scale_colour_gradientn("Seats\nper observation", 
                         colours=c( "#f9f3c2","#660000"))+ # change color scale
  coord_equal(ratio=1) + # square plot to avoid the distortion 
  theme(panel.background = element_blank()) #legend.position="none",
```
